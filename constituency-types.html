About Constituency Types

In India's electoral system, constituencies are categorized into three main types:

  • General: Open to all candidates
  • SC (Scheduled Caste): Reserved for SC candidates
  • ST (Scheduled Tribe): Reserved for ST candidates

This analysis explores how these different types of constituencies have voted over the years and which parties have performed best in each category.
