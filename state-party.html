About State-Party Analysis

This tool allows you to explore how different political parties perform in specific states over time. You can analyze:

  • Regional party strengths
  • How party support varies across different states
  • Changes in regional voting patterns over election cycles
  • State-specific political landscapes

Select a state to see performance of all major parties in that state, or select both state and party to focus on a specific party's performance.
