About This Application

This application allows you to explore the results of Lok Sabha elections in India from 1952 to 2024. You can:

Election Results

Look up the results of a specific constituency in a specific election year

Party Trends

View the trend of a party's performance across all election years

Party Comparison

Compare multiple parties' performances across all election years

The data used in this application is sourced from the Election Commission of India and other reliable sources.

New Feature: You can now compare multiple parties using the Party Comparison tool!
